The Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands

The Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, or the CNMI for short, is a U.S. territory located in the western Pacific Ocean. It is made up of 14 islands, with Saipan, Tinian, and Rota being where most of the population resides. Many people have never heard about the CNMI, which is rich in culture, history, and natural attractions. This website aims to help share information about the CNMI and give the islands more recognition and visibility on the web.
